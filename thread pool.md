 #os 
A thread pool maintains multiple threads waiting for tasks to be allocated for concurrent ([[concurrency]]) execution by the supervising program. 

By maintaining a pool of [[thread]]s, the model increases performance and avoids latency in execution due to frequent creation and destruction of threads for short-lived tasks. !
